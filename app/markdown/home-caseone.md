Nestled high in the majestic Sentinel Range, Mountain Tourist Resort is a year-round alpine retreat blending raw natural beauty with refined luxury. Spanning over 500 hectares of pristine wilderness, this exclusive sanctuary offers a complete escape where the mountains become the heart of your experience.

### Scenic Arrival and Four-Season Magic

The winding access road through ancient pine and larch forests reveals breathtaking vistas of turquoise lakes and jagged granite peaks.

- **Summer** — Wildflower meadows explode in vibrant color  
- **Autumn** — Larch forests turn gold against deep blue skies  
- **Winter** — A sparkling white wonderland with over 120 km of perfectly groomed slopes, high-speed lifts, and vast off-piste powder fields

### Luxurious Accommodations

#### Classic Timber Chalets
Hand-crafted from local stone and aged timber, featuring crackling fireplaces, fully equipped kitchens, spacious living areas, and private balconies with panoramic views. Perfect for families or groups.

#### Peakview Suites
Contemporary minimalist design with floor-to-ceiling windows framing the mountains. Polished stone, rich walnut finishes, luxurious wool throws, deep-soaking tubs, and private rooftop hot tubs for stargazing.

### Exceptional High-Altitude Dining

- **Granite Peak Restaurant**  
  Fine dining with a “peak-to-plate” philosophy: seasonal menus featuring foraged wild herbs, organic local produce, and fresh glacial-stream trout.

- **Starlight Lodge**  
  Cozy evenings of traditional Swiss fondue, curated European wine tastings, and open-air barbecues beneath the Milky Way.

### Adventure in Every Season

**Summer & Autumn**  
Hiking & e-bike trails, guided via ferrata, rock climbing, paragliding, stand-up paddleboarding, kayaking, and wild swimming in crystal-clear alpine lakes.

**Winter & Spring**  
World-class skiing & snowboarding, off-piste adventures, snowshoeing through silent forests, husky sledding, and ice-climbing on frozen waterfalls.

### The Alpine Sanctuary Wellness Center

An award-winning spa inspired by the surrounding peaks. Enjoy indoor-outdoor infinity pools that blend with the horizon, herbal treatments using local alpine plants, panoramic saunas, steam rooms, and sunrise yoga in a glass-walled studio overlooking the summits.

Whether you crave adrenaline, deep relaxation, or simply the purest mountain air, Mountain Tourist Resort delivers an unforgettable journey into the soul of the Alps — leaving every guest renewed long after they descend.
